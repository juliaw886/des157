Topic 1 | Fast Fashion

This is a trendy site that publicizes a protest against fast fashion that will be held this year. I chose this site because I think it utilizes lots of great website design and graphic design to create a site that is appealing to people of backgrounds. Specifically, it makes the protest seem “cool” to people who are unfamiliar to the cause and also probably makes them want to learn more about it. The interface is easy to use, with a navigation bar at the top of the page that shows a drop-down menu once you hover over it. The font is very easy to use and bold, so that your eye immediately goes to the right bit of information.
